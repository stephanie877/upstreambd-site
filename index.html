Common Questions

What people ask before booking a call.

How long does setup take?

Most systems are fully built and running within 30 days of the kickoff call. We move fast because we have done this before.

Do we need technical knowledge to use it?

No. We build everything and train your team. You do not touch the infrastructure. It is designed to run whether or not anyone actively maintains it.

How is this different from a CRM like Salesforce?

A CRM is a tool. This is a system. A CRM requires someone to update it — this updates itself. A CRM tracks what happened — this tells you what to do next. We have heard "we tried a CRM and nobody used it" from almost every firm we talk to. That is exactly the problem we solve.

What if our team does not adopt it?

That is the most common concern and the one we take most seriously. We build systems around how your team actually works — not how we wish they worked. The whole point is that it runs in the background whether or not people actively maintain it.

What does the guarantee actually mean?

If your system is not generating value — real, measurable value in your BD process — within 30 days of handoff, we keep working until it does. At no additional cost. We are confident enough in what we build to stand behind it.

Is this only for large firms?

It works best for firms of 10 to 200 people where BD is a real function but not a fully staffed department. If you have a dedicated BD person or principal carrying BD, this is built for you.
